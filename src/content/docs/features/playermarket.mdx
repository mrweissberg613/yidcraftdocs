Player Markets allow you to rent your own shop where you can display and sell items to other players.

---

## Renting a Shop

Head to the **Rent a Shop** building at spawn.

![Rent a Shop Building](/assets/playermarket-building.png)

Inside the building, click on an available shop sign to rent it.

![Shop Rental Sign](/assets/playermarket-sign.png)

- Rental Cost: **500 Shekels**
- Rental Duration: **4 real-life days**

:::caution[Rental Expiration]
If you do not renew your rental before it expires, your shop will be reset.

All items stored inside the shop will be permanently deleted.
:::

---

## Your Shop

Once you've rented a shop, you can decorate it and begin selling items.
---

## Managing Your Shop

You can:

- Stock items
- Decorate your shop
- Add members
- Transfer ownership
- Sell the shop back to the server

---

## Commands

| Command | Description |
| --- | --- |
| `/arm addmember <player>` | Give another player access to your shop. |
| `/arm offer <player> <price>` | Offer to sell your shop to another player. |
| `/armsellback` | Return your shop to the server. No refund is given. |

---

## Tips

:::tip
Well-organized shops attract more customers.
:::

:::note
Renew your shop before it expires to avoid losing your items.
:::
