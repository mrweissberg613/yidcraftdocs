# What Is the Kosher System?

The Kosher System rewards players for eating kosher foods and following kosher dietary rules. When you choose to keep kosher, you'll earn points by eating approved foods and unlock rewards as you progress.

:::note
Keeping kosher is optional, but it provides useful rewards. Eating non-kosher foods will result in strikes, so be careful what you eat.
:::

## How to Start Keeping Kosher

Run the following command:

```bash
/kosher keep
```

You will receive a confirmation message in chat. Once enabled, eating approved kosher foods will earn you points.

## Checking Your Status

To view your current progress, run:

```bash
/kosher status
```

This command shows:

- Your current points
- Your strike count
- Your next reward
- Any active cooldowns

## Rewards

Earn points by eating kosher foods and unlock rewards as you progress.
- **100 Points → 50 Shekels**
- **500 Points → 200 Shekels**
- **1000 Points → 500 Shekels**
- **5000 Points →  1500 Shekels**
- **10000 Points → 3500 Shekels**

Additional rewards may be added in future updates.

## Notifications

By default, the kosher system sends a notification whenever you earn points.

To toggle notifications on or off, run:

```bash
/kosher notifications
```

## Non-Kosher Foods

Eating any of the following foods will give you a strike:

- Beef (Raw or Cooked)
- Chicken (Raw or Cooked)
- Mutton (Raw or Cooked)
- Porkchop (Raw or Cooked)
- Rotten Flesh
- Spider Eye
- Pufferfish

:::caution
After receiving **3 strikes**, your kosher status will be removed. You must wait **4 days** before you can start keeping kosher again.
:::

## How to Get Kosher Meat

In real life, obtaining kosher meat involves a special method of slaughter called **Shechita**, which is performed according to Jewish dietary laws.

On Yidcraft, we've simplified this process while keeping it inspired by the real Halacha (Jewish law).

### Step 1: Craft a Shechita Knife
Before you can obtain kosher meat, you must craft a **Shechita Knife**.

### Step 2: Slaughter a Kosher Animal

Use the Shechita Knife to kill one of the following kosher animals:

- Cow
- Sheep
- Chicken

### Step 3: Collect Raw Kosher Meat

Animals killed with a Shechita Knife will drop **Raw Kosher Meat** instead of regular meat.

> **Note:** If an animal is killed without a Shechita Knife, it will not drop kosher meat.

### Step 4: Cook the Meat

Place the Raw Kosher Meat in a furnace, smoker, or other cooking device to receive **Cooked Kosher Meat**.
