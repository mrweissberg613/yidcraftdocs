# Creating a Player Shop

Chest Shops allow players to buy and sell items directly from a chest.

## Step 1: Place a Chest

Place a chest where you want your shop to be located.

## Step 2: Place a Sign

Place a sign exactly **one block away** from the chest (for example, directly above it).

Write the following format on the sign:

![Chest Shop Format](/assets/chestshopformat.png)

### Sign Format

**Line 1**
- Leave this line blank.
- The plugin will automatically fill it with your username.
- Players with the `ChestShop.admin` permission can create shops under other players' names.

**Line 2**
- Enter the quantity of items being bought or sold.

**Line 3**
- Enter the buy and/or sell price.
- Use:
  - `B` = Price players pay to **buy** items.
  - `S` = Price players receive when they **sell** items.
- Separate buy and sell prices with a colon (`:`).

Examples:

```text
B 5:5 S
B 5
S free
B 10:5 S
```

**Line 4**
- Enter the item name or ID.
- You can also place a `?` on this line and the plugin will automatically detect the item from the chest.
- Alternatively, click the sign while holding the desired item.
- Use `/iteminfo` to view the correct item name for the item you're holding.

## Example Shop

![Example Chest Shop](/assets/chestsshopexample.png)

### What This Shop Does

In the example above:

- Players can **buy 64 diamonds** for **$10**.
- Players can **sell 64 diamonds** back for **$5**.
- The shop owner is **Phoenix616**.

## Tips

:::tip
Make sure the chest contains enough stock before opening your shop to other players.
:::

:::note
If a shop is out of stock, players will not be able to purchase items from it.
:::
