## Welcome to Yidcraft

Welcome to Yidcraft! We are a warm, Jewish-friendly Minecraft community where players can build, explore, and connect together.

This wiki is the official place to learn about the server, the community, and how to join.

## What to do next

- Read the **How to join** page for the server IP.
- Explore the **Contribute** page if you want to help improve the wiki.
- See the **Trading** section in [Basics](/guides/basics/#trading) for how to trade items and shekels.
