## 1. Be Respectful
Treat everyone with kindness. No bullying, insults, harassment, or offensive comments.

Jokes and friendly banter are allowed, but anything that targets a person or crosses into real-life harm is not.

Any form of prejudice, including racism, sexism, or religious discrimination, will result in an immediate ban.

## 2. Protect the Spirit of the Server
This server exists for fun and community. Do not intentionally ruin the experience for other players.

## 3. No Spam
Avoid excessive or repetitive messages. Keep chat clean and enjoyable for everyone.

## 4. No Exploiting Bugs
If you discover a bug, report it immediately.

Abusing glitches or unintended mechanics will result in punishment.

## 5. Totem Farming
Automated farming of Totems of Undying is not allowed. Totems are intended for fair progression.

## ✡️ Jewish Server Guidelines

### Shabbat and Holidays
The server may be closed on Shabbat and Yom Tov. Use this time to rest, disconnect, and focus on real life.

### Respect in Speech (Derech Eretz)
Always speak respectfully. Avoid insults, toxic behavior, or inappropriate language.

### Fairness and Integrity
Play honestly. Cheating, deception, or gaining unfair advantages is not allowed. Progress should come from your own effort.
