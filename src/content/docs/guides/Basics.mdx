**Welcome to Yidcraft!** This guide covers everything you need to know when joining the server for the first time.

## Your first spawn

When you join the server, you will appear in <Badge text="Jerusalem" variant="success" />, the central hub city. You will spawn on the **central island**.

On the island, you will see a hologram pointing to the **Mining Island** one of the best ways to earn your first shekels on the server.

## Mining Island

The Mining Island is a trading hub designed for new players to learn the economy:

- **Mine ores** and sell them to earn currency
- **Buy ores or materials** from island shops
- **Learn prices** and develop trading strategies

:::tip[Economy tip]
Instead of using coins as the currenecy we use something called shekels
:::

## Essential commands

Two commands help you navigate quickly:

```text
/town
```
Travel to <Badge text="Jerusalem" variant="success" /> from anywhere on the server.

```text
/spawn
```
Return to the central island spawn when already in Jerusalem.

:::info[Navigation]
If you get lost, `/town` will always bring you back to the city center.
:::

## Trading

The Shekels system is how Yidcraft handles currency. You can trade items and currency with other players directly using the trade system.

### How to initiate a trade

1. Find another player you want to trade with
2. Run the command:
   ```text
   /trade otherplayer
   ```
3. Replace `otherplayer` with their exact username

### Trade menu walkthrough

Once the trade is initiated:

1. The other player receives a request and can **accept** or **deny** it
2. If accepted, a trade menu opens showing both inventories
3. Each player can:
   - Place items into their **trade inventory** (left side)
   - Set the number of Shekels they are offering (top)
4. View what the other player is offering on the right side
5. **Accept** or **deny** the trade offer
6. If both players accept, a countdown timer starts
7. When the countdown finishes, the trade completes automatically

:::caution[Trade confirmation]
Make sure you agree with the trade before accepting. Once both players confirm and the countdown begins, the trade cannot be cancelled!
:::

### Trading tips

- Always verify the items and currency amounts before accepting
- Be cautious of suspicious trade requests
- Ask in chat if you are unsure about a fair trade value
