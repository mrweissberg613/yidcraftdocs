---

## Your First Join

When you first join the server, you will spawn at the main spawn area.

From here, you can begin exploring the world, learn the server's features, and start earning your first shekels.

---

## Earning Shekels

There are many ways to make money on YidCraft, including:

- ⛏️ Mining and selling ores
- 🎣 Fishing
- 🛒 Creating Chest Shops
- 🥩 Selling kosher foods
- 📈 Investing in the stock market
- 🌾 Farming and gathering resources

As more features are added, even more ways to earn shekels will become available.

:::tip[Currency]
YidCraft uses **Shekels** as its primary currency instead of coins.
:::

## Essential Commands

The main command you'll need when getting started is:

```text
/spawn
```

Returns you to the server spawn from almost anywhere.

:::info[Tip]
If you ever get lost, simply use `/spawn` to return to the main spawn area.
:::

## Trading

Players can safely trade both items and shekels using the built-in trading system.

### Starting a Trade

Find the player you'd like to trade with and run:

```text
/trade <player>
```

Replace `<player>` with their Minecraft username.

### How Trading Works

1. Send a trade request.
2. The other player accepts or declines.
3. If accepted, the trading menu opens.
4. Place your items into the trade window.
5. Add the amount of shekels you wish to offer.
6. Review the other player's offer.
7. Both players click **Accept**.
8. After a short countdown, the trade is completed automatically.

:::caution[Trade Carefully]
Always double-check the items and shekel amounts before accepting. Once both players confirm and the countdown finishes, the trade cannot be reversed.
:::

### Trading Tips

- Verify every item before accepting.
- Confirm the amount of shekels being traded.
- Be cautious of unexpected trade requests.
- Ask other players if you're unsure about an item's value.
